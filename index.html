About the Album

Kid Cudi's Man on the Moon: The End of Day album was relaeased on September 15th of 2009. This album was the first installment of the Man on the Moon trilogy. On this album some of the most popular songs were Day n Nite, Make Her Say and Pursuit of Happiness. It was became certified four-times platinum by the RIAA and was ranked in the 500 greatest albums of all time.

Mind Barriers

A big theme of the Album relates to mental health. Understanding how these barriers can affect your mind is the first step to free your mind!

Grief

Grief significantly impacts the brain by triggering the release of stress hormones, which can disrupt cognitive functions like memory, concentration, and decision-making, often leading to "brain fog".

Depression

Depression affects the brain by causing structural changes, particularly in areas related to memory, decision-making, and emotions, which can manifest as difficulties with concentration, memory, mood regulation, and overall cognitive function

Anxiety

Anxiety primarily affects the brain by hyperactivating the limbic system, particularly the amygdala, which is responsible for processing fear, leading to increased emotional responses, and potential disruptions in memory and cognitive function

Loneliness

Loneliness can significantly affect the brain in areas that control social cognition, emotion processing, and stress response, leading to changes in how people perceive social interactions

Alcoholism

Alcoholism significantly impacts the brain, leading to impairments in memory, decision-making, balance, coordination, and overall cognitive function

Family Issues

Family issues can significantly affect brain development by impacting the structure and function of areas related to stress regulation, emotion processing, and social interaction
