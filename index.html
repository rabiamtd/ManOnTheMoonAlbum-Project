About the Album

The 15th Anniversary Edition of 'Man on The Moon: Free Your Mind' takes inspiration from Kid Cudi’s 'Day and Night,' exploring themes of duality and contrast. The album cover is designed in black and white to stand out, while vibrant colors are used throughout the campaign to create contrast. Fractals and space influence the visuals, reflecting the complexity of the mind and the vastness of the universe.
